## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Navigate to the **SocialPulse** login/registration page.
2. Enter your details (Email, Password, Full Name) or use an invite link if you are joining an existing team.
3. Upon registration, you will be placed on the **Free** plan by default, giving you access to basic features and a starting balance of AI credits.

### Basic Setup
* **Profile Settings**: Head over to the **Settings** or **Profile** tab to update your name, change your password, and configure your notification preferences (e.g., getting alerts for failed posts or weekly digests).

---

## 2. Connecting Your Social Accounts

Before you can publish anything, you need to connect your social media profiles.

1. Go to the **Settings** > **Connected Accounts** section (or click on the platform icons in the Content Studio).
2. You can connect accounts from:
   * **Twitter/X**
   * **Instagram** (Business/Creator accounts recommended)
   * **LinkedIn**
   * **Facebook** (Pages/Groups)
3. Click **Connect** next to the desired platform. You will be redirected to the platform's official OAuth authorization page.
4. Grant the necessary permissions and you will be redirected back to SocialPulse.

> **Note**: The number of accounts you can connect depends on your billing plan (Free: 2 accounts, Starter: 5, Pro: 15, Enterprise: Unlimited).

## 3. Navigating the Dashboard

The **Dashboard** is your central command center. When you log in, you will see:
* **Greeting & AI Credits**: A quick view of your remaining AI credits for the month.
* **Stat Cards**: High-level metrics showing your total audience, recent engagement, and publishing volume.
* **Recent Posts**: A feed of your most recently published or upcoming scheduled posts.
* **Navigation Sidebar**: Quick access to Content Studio, Scheduler, Analytics, Media Library, and more.

---

## 4. The Content Studio & AI Tools

The **Content Studio** is where the magic happens. Here you craft your posts using powerful AI integrations.

### Writing a Post
1. Select the platform(s) you want to post to.
2. Type your content in the text editor. A character counter will help you stay within platform limits.
3. Add media from your **Media Library** or upload directly.

### Using AI Features
SocialPulse is integrated with OpenAI's GPT-4 and DALL-E 3 to turbocharge your content creation:
* **AI Writer**: Give the AI a topic, tone, length, and target audience. It will generate a complete post for you.
* **Improve Content**: Have a draft? The AI can rewrite it to be more engaging, professional, or funny.
* **AI Hashtags**: Automatically generate relevant and trending hashtags based on your topic.
* **AI Captions**: Upload an image, and the AI will analyze it to write a perfect caption.
* **AI Image Generation**: Use DALL-E 3 to generate custom images based on text prompts directly within the app.

## 5. Scheduling & Publishing

Once your content is ready, you have several options:

### Publish Now
Hit **Publish Now** to immediately push the post to your selected platforms.

### Schedule for Later
1. Toggle the **Schedule** option.
2. Select a date and time.
3. Click **Schedule Post**. The post will be added to your queue and published automatically.

### Bulk Scheduler
Have a lot of content? Use the **Bulk Scheduler** (available on Pro/Enterprise plans):
* Upload a CSV or use the UI to schedule multiple posts at once.
* Great for planning a whole month of content in one sitting.

## 6. Media Library & Image Editor

The **Media Library** stores all your images and videos in the cloud (AWS S3 or Cloudinary).

* **Uploading**: Drag and drop files to upload. Support for images and videos (size limits apply based on your plan).
* **Organizing**: Tag your media or organize them into folders.
* **Image Editor**: Click "Edit" on any image to open the built-in Canvas editor. You can crop, add filters, add text, undo/redo, and save the modified image back to your library.

---

## 7. Analytics & Reporting

Track your success in the **Analytics** tab.

* **Overview Metrics**: See total likes, comments, shares, impressions, reach, and clicks across all connected platforms.
* **Platform Breakdown**: Filter analytics by a specific platform (e.g., just Twitter).
* **Date Ranges**: Analyze data over the last 7, 14, 30, or 90 days.
* **Engagement Rate**: Automatically calculates how well your audience is interacting with your content.

*Data is synced automatically in the background, ensuring you always have up-to-date insights.*

## 8. Advanced Features

For power users, SocialPulse offers advanced tools to automate and monitor your social presence:

### Social Listening
* Track specific keywords, brand names, or hashtags across platforms.
* View results in the app to see what people are saying about your brand in real-time.

### Unified Inbox
* Stop switching between apps! The Unified Inbox pulls in all your mentions, comments, and DMs.
* Read and mark messages as read across all platforms from one screen.

### RSS Auto-Posting
* Connect RSS feeds from your favorite blogs or news sites.
* SocialPulse can automatically fetch new articles and post them to your social accounts at specific intervals.

## 9. Workspaces & Teams

Working with others or managing multiple brands?

### Workspaces
* Switch between different Workspaces using the dropdown in the top navbar.
* Each Workspace can have its own branding (custom colors) and associated social accounts.

### Teams (Collaboration)
* Invite colleagues or clients to your team via email.
* Assign roles (Owner, Admin, Member, Viewer) to control who can publish content or manage billing.

## 10. Account & Billing

Manage your subscription from the **Billing** tab.

* **Plans**: Upgrade from Free to Starter, Pro, or Enterprise to unlock higher limits (more accounts, more posts/month, more AI credits).
* **Usage Bar**: Visually track your current month's usage against your plan limits.
* **Customer Portal**: Access the Stripe billing portal to update payment methods, download invoices, or cancel your subscription.
* **Referrals**: Share your unique referral code to earn bonus AI credits when friends sign up!
